Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

At SwiftCounter, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we handle data in our mobile application.

1. Subscription & Payments

SwiftCounter offers a "Pro" membership. All payment transactions are processed securely through the Apple App Store. We do not collect or store your credit card or financial information on our servers.

2. Advertising & Data Collection

The free version of SwiftCounter displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. Our third-party advertising partners may collect and use your device's advertising identifier (such as IDFA on iOS) to provide personalized ads. On iOS devices, we request your permission to track this data via the App Tracking Transparency framework.

3. Local Image Processing

Any image or camera-based counting happens locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or share your photos or camera feed with any external servers. Your visual data never leaves your device.

4. Children's Privacy

SwiftCounter does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at: kaptangames.official@gmail.com
